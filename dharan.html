Dharan - The Gateway to Eastern Nepal

Dharan is a vibrant city in eastern Nepal, famous for its diverse community, scenic beauty, and bustling market areas.

Major Castes in Dharan

    Rai

    Rai people are an indigenous ethnic group from eastern Nepal, known for their bravery and service in the military. They have a rich cultural heritage and celebrate various traditional festivals.

    Limbu

    Limbu people are another prominent ethnic group in eastern Nepal. They are known for their rich culture, traditional attire, and vibrant festivals like Udhauli and Ubhauli.

    Tamang

    Tamang people are an important ethnic group in eastern Nepal, known for their unique language, culture, and traditional dances. They celebrate festivals like Lhosar with great enthusiasm.

Major Festivals in Dharan

    Maghe Sankranti

    Celebrated in January, this festival marks the winter solstice. Families gather to enjoy traditional foods like sesame seed sweets and yam.

    Dantakali Jatra

    This festival is dedicated to the goddess Dantakali, celebrated with a grand procession and traditional rituals in the Dantakali Temple.

    Buddha Jayanti

    Celebrating the birth, enlightenment, and death of Lord Buddha, this festival is marked by prayers, meditation, and processions in various monasteries and stupas.

Traditional Foods of Traditional Castes

    Rai Food

    Rai cuisine includes traditional dishes like ‘wachipa’ (rice with chicken and herbs), ‘masikau’ (smoked meat), and various fermented foods and pickles.

    Limbu Food

    Limbu cuisine features dishes like ‘sishnu’ (nettle soup), ‘tongba’ (millet beer), and ‘kinema’ (fermented soybean curry).

    Tamang Food

    Tamang cuisine is known for dishes like ‘sel roti’ (rice flour doughnut), ‘aloo tama’ (potato and bamboo shoot curry), and ‘yak meat’ dishes.

Cultural Sites in Dharan

    Dantakali Temple

    Dantakali Temple is a sacred Hindu temple dedicated to the goddess Dantakali. It is known for its religious significance and beautiful surroundings.

    Buddha Subba Temple

    Buddha Subba Temple is a famous religious site, believed to fulfill the wishes of its devotees. It is surrounded by bamboo groves and holds cultural significance.

    Bhedetar

    Bhedetar is a hill station near Dharan, known for its stunning views and pleasant climate. It is a popular destination for both locals and tourists.
